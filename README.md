# Alterrell Interactive & Obsidian Futures

This repository houses the code for [interactive.alterrell.com](https://interactive.alterrell.com), a platform dedicated to data-driven civic storytelling, historical analysis, and interactive essays. 

This is a non-commercial, volunteer-led project. It is supported by community tips via [Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/alterrell).

## 📊 Current & In-Progress Projects

### Alterrell Interactive: Civic & Structural Analysis
* **Fast Food's Hidden Sodium Tax:** An interactive exploration of how zip-code targeted advertising and nutritional architecture contribute to localized healthcare crises.
* **Harvard Business School & Race:** A data-backed look at the structural incentives within elite business institutions regarding the racial wealth and opportunity gap.
* **Congressional Selfishness:** An analysis of the growing gap between Congressional benefits (salary, retirement, and age) and the economic reality of the American workforce.
* **Who Do We Call During a Disaster:** Investigating the data behind government disaster response and the shifting expectations of public safety.

### ✦ Obsidian Futures: Culture & Data
* **Black Music Royalty:** Mapping decades of cultural influence, industry power, and the evolution of musical lineage.
* **What's in a Name:** A data reveal exploring the history, aspirations, and identity shifts found in naming conventions over time.
