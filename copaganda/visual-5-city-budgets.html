New York vs. Norway

The NYPD patrols 304 square miles and 8.5 million people. Norway defends 385,000 square kilometers of land, nearly 2 million square kilometers of sea territory, and has NATO obligations against a nuclear neighbor.

Same budget.

Dallas vs. Croatia

Dallas PD and Croatia's entire military cost roughly the same to run. Croatia has fighter jets, a navy, and defense obligations to 30 NATO allies.

Dallas has 385 square miles.
