NBC + CBS

NBC and CBS account for the vast majority of producer-attributed cop show seasons. ABC carried the genre in the 1970s through Spelling, then ceded the field. Nickelodeon is the only non-broadcast entry—and it’s a children’s show.

Network attribution follows original broadcast network for each show. T.J. Hooker aired on both ABC and CBS; counted here as ABC (majority of run). PAW Patrol counted under Nickelodeon. Source: IMDb, Wikipedia.
