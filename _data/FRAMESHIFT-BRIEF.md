## THESIS
Your body data belongs to you.
FrameShift is a browser-native health tool that gives
people the context their doctor doesn't have time to provide —
without storing anything, without judging anything,
and without pretending to be a replacement for care.

## LOCKED DECISIONS
- "Sex assigned at birth" language with explanatory note
- Imperial/metric toggle
- Inclusive measurement instructions (no gendered landmarks)
- Threshold language: "Within range / Approaching threshold /
  Above clinical threshold" (not clinical labels)
- Visual range bars
- Batch lab entry by test date
- Time horizon dropdown
- Four delivery methods: clipboard, email, SMS, PDF
- Zero data storage / zero PII collected
- User downloads JSON, re-uploads to continue
- Demand validation via waitlist + refundable deposit
  before paid tier build
- Launch tied to "Being Fat" editorial piece
- Legal review: cut exposure rather than hedge
