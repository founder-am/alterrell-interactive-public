## OPENING SCENE (locked framing)

AMA has gained weight. The rules of dating and desire that were true when he was thinner no longer apply. The explicit exclusions ("no fats, no fems") went underground — the language cleaned up, the behavior didn't. Part of him misses the clarity of those times, while acknowledging the privilege that clarity represented. Some readers were socialized fat and never had that access. The piece names this honestly and early. The narrator's partial vantage point is an asset, not a liability — he can see both sides of the wall.

Disability analogy: acquired vs. congenital. The person who becomes deaf as an adult grieves what they lost and learns to see the hearing world's assumptions from outside. Same move here.
