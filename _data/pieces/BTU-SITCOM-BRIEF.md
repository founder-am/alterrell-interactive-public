## ELIGIBILITY RULES (LOCKED)

### Primary Tier (80%+ Black cast)
An actor qualifies for the BTU sitcom cluster Primary Tier if they meet either:
- Main or recurring cast on **1 Black sitcom for 3+ seasons**, OR
- Recurring cast on **2+ Black sitcoms** regardless of season count

"Black sitcom" = scripted comedy with 80%+ Black cast.

### Secondary Tier (Black lead, mixed cast)
An actor qualifies for Secondary Tier if:
- They were the **Black lead** on a scripted comedy with a mixed cast
- The show ran **3+ seasons**
- The actor received an **Emmy nomination or NAACP Image Award nomination** in a lead or supporting comedy category for that role

Secondary tier actors appear in the network but with a distinct visual marker (lighter node, asterisk label). They are connected to Primary nodes via verified shared cast appearances only.

### NAACP Tiebreaker
For any actor who doesn't clearly meet episode thresholds, an NAACP Image Award nomination or win for that show is sufficient to confirm inclusion.

### What does NOT qualify
- Reality television (scripted only)
- Guest appearances of fewer than 3 episodes on a single show
- Film credits (TV only for this cluster)
