## PIECE CONTEXT

The BTU Soap Opera Cluster (Cluster D) is a **separate visual cluster** within the BTU network. Soaps operate on different logic than scripted dramas and sitcoms — actors often appear for decades on a single show, episode counts reach into the thousands, and the same actors migrate across multiple soap properties as shows get cancelled and casts disperse.

This cluster has not yet had a dedicated research pass. This brief is a starter document to lock the eligibility rules and confirm the known anchor shows before that pass begins.

---

## ELIGIBILITY RULES (LOCKED)

### Primary Tier
An actor qualifies for the BTU Soap Cluster Primary Tier if they meet either:
- Main or recurring cast on **1 soap opera for 3+ seasons**, OR
- Recurring cast on **2+ soap operas** regardless of season count

"Soap opera" = daily or weekly serialized drama, produced for daytime or primetime, running in ongoing open-ended format.

### NAACP Tiebreaker
NAACP Image Award nomination or win for a soap role is sufficient to confirm inclusion for borderline actors. The NAACP Image Awards have a dedicated Daytime Drama category — this is the primary validation source for this cluster.

### What does NOT qualify
- Reality programming
- Scripted primetime dramas with soap-like serialization (Power, Empire) — those belong in Cluster B
- Guest appearances under 3 episodes
