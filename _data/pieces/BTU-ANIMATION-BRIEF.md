## PIECE CONTEXT

The BTU Animation Cluster (Cluster E) is a **secondary cluster** — it is part of the BTU but visually subordinate to Clusters A–D. It covers scripted animated series with majority-Black casts or majority-Black creative leadership. Voice acting is the qualifying credit type.

This cluster is lower priority than Clusters A–D. It is documented here to lock the eligibility rules before a future research pass.

---

## ELIGIBILITY RULES (LOCKED)

### Secondary status
The Animation Cluster is Secondary by definition. All nodes in this cluster carry the secondary visual marker regardless of episode count.

### Qualifying criteria
An actor qualifies for the BTU Animation Cluster if they meet either:
- Main or recurring **voice cast** on **1 Black animated series for 3+ seasons**, OR
- Recurring voice cast on **2+ Black animated series** regardless of season count

"Black animated series" = animated scripted series with either:
- 80%+ Black voice cast, OR
- Black creator/showrunner AND majority-Black subject matter

### What does NOT qualify
- Reality programming
- Non-animated sketch comedy
- Voice work in non-BTU-eligible animated series (e.g., a Black actor voicing a character in a non-Black animated show)
