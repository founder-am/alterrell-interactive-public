## CHART DIRECTION STANDARDS (LOCKED)

### Card parity rule (ALL types)
Every card requires a full 3-stat row. No empty stat slots. Type C (revival stories — Viola, Zora) use multiplier in stat 3 rather than leaving it blank. Bottom block treatment varies by type but stat row is always complete.

### Direction 1 — Cropped Window
**Use for:** Pure spike stories — Kobe, Halle, Aaliyah, Tyra. Zero-to-spike drama.
**How:** Crop x-axis to ±5 years around the action. Cliff at left edge is the argument.
**Stats block:** 3-stat row (peak count, multiplier, key year) + 1-in-X comparable below.

### Direction 2 — Dual Panel
**Use for:** Long-arc or slow-build stories — Maya, Zora, Denzel, Viola, Colson.
**How:** Full x-axis kept. Pre-period line is muted/dashed. Post-period line is gold/active.
**Stats block:** Same 3-stat row. 1-in-X comparable where ratio is meaningful; revival framing where ratio is weak (Viola, Zora).

### Direction 3 — Multi Indexed
**Use for:** Grouped charts — athletes, Arab names, gender divide.
**How:** Indexed to debut year = 100. Each name gets its own color. Legend shows name + peak index.
**Stats block:** Replace 1-in-X with insight callout. The comparison between lines IS the argument.
