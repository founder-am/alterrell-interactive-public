Layer 0 — Before you decide anything
Governance first
What does "done" mean for a piece?
The platform has no shared definition. Multiple pieces are "built" but editorially incomplete.
Decisions needed
  • Define the 5 gates a piece must pass: Brief locked → Data audited → Copy voice-passed → Build complete → Deploy checklist green
  • No piece can be called live until all 5 gates pass
  • This gate definition becomes the new PROJECT-STATUS.md schema
Governance first
Which pieces are active vs. parked?
Right now, 8+ pieces compete for attention with no triage order.
Decisions needed
  • Enforce a hard two-piece limit: one in active build, one in active editorial
  • Everything else is Parked — no sessions opened, no briefs modified
  • Parked pieces get a one-line status note; nothing more until unparked

Layer 2 — Role audit
Role active
EIC
Mobilization, urgency, calendar ownership. Works for single-piece. Needs scope at portfolio scale.
Gap to address
  • EIC currently has no authority to pause or park a piece — that's a new responsibility
  • EIC should own the two-piece limit: nothing moves to active without EIC sign-off
Role active
CBUXO
Design system, emotional arc, analytical literacy check. Works per piece.
Gap to address
  • CBUXO has no cross-piece role — no one is checking whether the hub as a whole reads for a non-analytical visitor
  • Should CBUXO sign off on hub layout changes the same way it does piece builds?
Role active
Sr. Data Architect
Session structure, briefs, context limits, handoffs. Pipeline enforcement.
Gap to address
  • Data Architect currently has no authority to reject a session opener that mixes session types — that needs to be an explicit call
  • No one owns PROJECT-STATUS.md updates between sessions — pieces drift in status
Role active
Editorial Partner
Voice pass, copy critique, thesis check. Active in Type 1 sessions only.
Gap to address
  • No role currently covers post-deploy editorial review — checking if a live piece is landing with readers
Role missing
Portfolio Manager
No role owns the full piece inventory, status triage, or debt tracking across all pieces.
What this role would own
  • Maintaining the active/parked/complete status of every piece
  • Flagging when a piece has been in the same status for 2+ sessions with no movement
  • Deciding when a parked piece gets unparked and why
  • Decision needed: Does this become a standing session opener role, or a scheduled quarterly review?
Role missing
QA / Reader Advocate
No one tests a piece as a cold reader before deploy. CBUXO flags risk; no one actually reads it cold.
What this role would own
  • A mandatory cold-read pass before every deploy: reads the piece as someone who has never heard of the platform
  • Asks three questions: Do I know what this is about in 5 seconds? Do I feel something in the first tab? Do I know what to do next?
  • This could be a Claude prompt mode or a human reader check — decision needed
Role missing
Platform Debt Owner
Known debt exists (nav contrast, scroll-spy retrofit, journey block links) with no owner and no timeline.
What this role would own
  • A running debt log with severity: blocks deploy / degrades experience / cosmetic
  • Ensures at least one Batch A (platform) session ships per quarter
  • Prevents debt from becoming invisible — it gets named, sized, and scheduled
