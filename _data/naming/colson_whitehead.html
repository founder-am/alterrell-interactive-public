The Pulitzer Effect

Before 2016, the name "Colson" barely existed. Then "The Underground Railroad" (2016 novel, 2017 Pulitzer) thrust Whitehead into national conversation—Oprah's Book Club, National Book Award, cultural phenomenon. The name jumped to 322 babies. Then "The Nickel Boys" (2019 novel, 2020 Pulitzer) cemented his genius. 654 babies. Naming your child "Colson" in 2019 means: "We value this Black intellectual who writes about racism with unflinching honesty."
