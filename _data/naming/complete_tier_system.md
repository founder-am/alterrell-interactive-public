## TISHA CAMPBELL - DETAILED ASSESSMENT

**Why she "feels important" but data doesn't validate:**

✅ **Cultural Significance:**
- Gina Waters-Payne was beloved character
- Martin was HUGE in Black community
- Peak Black sitcom era (1992-1997)
- She had sustained career (My Wife & Kids 2001-2005)

❌ **Data Reality:**
- Peak: 868 in **1971** (age 3! Before any career)
- 1990-1992 (pre-Martin): 122 → 102 → 111
- 1992-1997 (Martin years): Average 89 (DECLINED)
- Post-Martin: Continued decline (43 → 37 → 30)

**Verdict:** 
- Name was already fading generationally
- Her fame couldn't reverse the trend
- This is actually a **counterexample** - shows fame alone can't save declining names
- OR: Shows "Tisha" was perceived as dated even as she was popular

**Use Case:**
- Could include as "Limits of Fame" example
- "Even beloved characters can't reverse generational naming trends"
