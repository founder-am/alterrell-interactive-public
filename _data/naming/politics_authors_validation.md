## KEY INSIGHTS

### Pattern 1: **"The Canon Effect"**
- Maya, Octavia, Zora all show spikes tied to being taught in schools/canon
- Literary legitimacy = name legitimacy

### Pattern 2: **"The Awards Effect"**  
- Colson's 2017-2019 spike directly tracks his back-to-back Pulitzers
- Awards = media coverage = mainstream acceptance

### Pattern 3: **"Posthumous Revival"**
- Octavia (died 2006, spiked 2017-2019)
- Zora (died 1960, spiked 2014-2019)
- Literary rediscovery creates delayed naming trends

### Pattern 4: **"Political Names Are Different"**
- Barack's 71 babies seems small, but represents INFINITE increase from baseline
- Political names may have smaller absolute numbers due to higher stakes/controversy
- But symbolic power is enormous
