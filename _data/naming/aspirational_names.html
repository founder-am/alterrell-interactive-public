Naming Strategy

ASPIRATIONAL

Luxury Names • Mixed Results

Some Black families chose names that signaled aspiration: Diamond, Princess, Mercedes, Lorenzo. Luxury goods, European sophistication, regal titles. A different strategy than traditional names or celebrity-driven choices.

The results were mixed. Diamond exploded 120× from 1975, peaking at 2,160 babies in 1999, then crashed back down. Mercedes had a similar arc—peaked in the 90s, then faded. Princess sustained steady growth, still strong in 2019.

But Lorenzo bucked every trend. Steady, sustained growth from 1975 to 2019, peaking at 2,630. No spike-and-crash. No celebrity driver. Just decades of families choosing Italian sophistication over flash.
