# THREE-BUCKET FRAMEWORK: Tier 1 Name Categorization

## THE THREE BUCKETS

### **BUCKET 1: MAINSTREAM TAKEOVER** 
*Large absolute numbers (1,000+ peak) + Crossover appeal*
- Both Black AND white families naming kids
- Media saturation, household name status
- Sports/music dominate this bucket

### **BUCKET 2: RISING AUTHORITY (Black Community)**
*High increase factors (10×+) but moderate absolute numbers (100-1,500 peak)*
- Primarily Black families
- Name becomes "legitimate" within community after fame
- TV/sitcoms dominate this bucket

### **BUCKET 3: BLACK CLASSICS**
*Persistent popularity post-1975, not tied to single celebrity*
- Established names in Black community
- Generational staying power
- May have celebrity associations but broader than one person

## KEY INSIGHTS BY BUCKET

### MAINSTREAM = Music + Sports
- Largest absolute numbers
- Crossover appeal (white + Black families)
- Immediate impact within 1-3 years

### BLACK AUTHORITY = TV + Authors + Politics
- High increase factors, moderate absolute numbers
- "Waiting to see" pattern (2-5 year lag)
- Cultural legitimacy within community
- Curriculum/education important (authors)
- TV creates shared Black viewing experience

### BLACK CLASSICS = Not Celebrity-Driven
- Generational patterns (what was cool in 1980 ≠ 2010)
- Established post-segregation
- May have celebrity association but broader
- Shows cultural baseline independent of fame
