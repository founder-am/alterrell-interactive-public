Half & Half as Crossroads

Connects 7 other BTU shows through its cast alone
Via Essence Atkins: Smart Guy, Are We There Yet?, Marlon (+ Cosby Show guest)
Via Telma Hopkins: Family Matters, Are We There Yet?
Via Yvette Lee Bowser (creator): also created Living Single
Half & Half connects to 7 shows through 2 cast members and 1 creator. It is the most connected show relative to its cast size in the BTU.

BTU Network Map — First Pass
This map is incomplete. It covers ~28 shows and ~45 actors. Major gaps: The Steve Harvey Show cast, The Jamie Foxx Show cast, One on One cast, The Game cast, Hangin' with Mr. Cooper cast, In the House cast, Roc cast, The Bernie Mac Show cast, House of Payne full cast, Insecure cast connections, Atlanta cast connections, Empire cast connections. Each of these shows likely adds new nodes and new connections to the network. The 90s UPN/WB cluster is especially underdeveloped — that era probably has the densest connections of any period in Black television.

Shows included must have 3+ seasons and be primarily Black-cast primetime television. Actors qualify with main or recurring roles (not single guest appearances). Decade started = decade of first qualifying BTU appearance.
