Methodology & Data Notes

All name frequency data is from the Social Security Administration's publicly available baby name dataset, which records names given to at least 5 babies per year in the United States. Data covers 1960–2019. Charts display raw counts per year. Annotation dates are derived from publicly available release, broadcast, and awards records.
